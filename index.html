Getting Started

Product Feeds

Product Feeds are the primary way to get products from Bonsai into your platform. They are updated frequently and contain all the information you need to sell products.

API

The Bonsai API is a RESTful API that allows you to get up-to-date product information, generate checkout totals and place orders. It is the most flexible way to integrate with Bonsai.

Images

Our image service allows you to fetch product images in various sizes. You can either use these directly to display them on your website, or reupload them to your own service.
